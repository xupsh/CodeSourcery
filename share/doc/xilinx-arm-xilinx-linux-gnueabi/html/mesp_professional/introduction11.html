Cycling Power to the System

When you need to apply or cycle power to the probe, connect or disconnect the power cable from the power source or from the probe. After you connect the probe to your target system, use the following sequence for applying or removing the power:

To turn the power on:

  1. Apply power to the probe.
  1. Apply power to the target system.

To turn the power off:

  1. Remove power from the target system.
  1. Remove power from the probe.
