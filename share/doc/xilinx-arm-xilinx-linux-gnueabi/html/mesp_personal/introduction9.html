Cycling Power to the System

When you need to apply or cycle power to the Sourcery Probe, connect or disconnect the power cable from the power source or from the probe. After you have connected the probe to your target system, perform the following steps to apply or remove the power.

Note

The first time you connect the probe to a PC, the PC will attempt to install and configure USB drivers. The requisite process is covered in the next chapter.

 

Procedure

To turn the power on, perform these steps:

  1. Connect the USB cable from the PC to the Sourcery Probe.
  1. Turn on the target system power.

To turn the power off, perform these steps:

  1. Turn off the target system power.
  1. Remove the USB cable from the Sourcery Probe.
