Connecting the Probe to the Target

This section explains how to connect a Sourcery Probe Personal to both your host computer and to the target.

Caution

Sourcery Probe Personal contains components that are subject to damage from electrostatic discharge. Whenever you are using, handling, or transporting the probe, or connecting to or disconnecting from a target system, always use proper anti-static protection measures, as specified in Standard Electrostatic Precautions.
